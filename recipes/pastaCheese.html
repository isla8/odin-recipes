Pasta and Cheese

Pasta and Cheese has always been my favourite food ever since I was young. This is my own recipe (if you can even call it that hehe).

Ingredients

  • As much pasta as you wish
  • Freshly grated cheese
  • (Optional) You may add sauces or herbs, anything that you think will make it taste better!

Steps

  1. Boil enough water so that all the pasta will be submerged
  2. When boiling, put in as much pasta as you desire
  3. Stir every few minutes to stop the pasta from sticking
  4. After about 8-10 minutes, check on how cooked the pasta is. If it is to your liking then take it off the heat and strain it
  5. Put the pasta on a plate or in a bowl
  6. Now grate cheese over the top (As much as you wish!)
  7. Enjoy!
